About Us

We are an organization by high school students for the new generation of programmers.

Based in NYC, we aim to bring together high school students from all different backgrounds that are interested in technology and programming.

We organize collaborative events where students can learn, connect, and build.

Connect

We connect and empower young hackers everywhere! Our events allows them to meet other programmers as well as our mentors and organizers.

Interact

We encourage everyone to meet and connect with new people at all of our events so they can build awesome projects together.

Inspire

We welcome programmers of all experience levels - we often have workshops at our hackathons to teach and inspire students to program.

Unite

We have a strong community of young programmers that we bring together in NYC. Join us by signing up for our mailing list here
